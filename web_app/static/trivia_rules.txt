Press 'One Player!' to have 5 random questions thrown at you.<br>
Answer fast to get the highest score!<br>
Your score is based on how fast you answer the question.<br>
<br>
Answer with 10 seconds left gives you 10 points.<br>
Answer with 3 seconds left gives you 3 points.<br>
<br>
Press 'Two Player!' to compete against another person.<br>
The game will wait until another player joins.<br>
You can compare scores after both players have finished<br>
