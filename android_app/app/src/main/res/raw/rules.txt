Press 'One Player!' to have 5 random questions thrown at you.
Answer fast to get the highest score!
Your score is based on how fast you answer the question.

Answer with 10 seconds left gives you 10 points.
Answer with 3 seconds left gives you 3 points.

Press 'Two Player!' to compete against another person.
The app will wait until another player joins.
You can compare scores after both players have finished.
